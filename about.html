About

The aim of fBay is to bring people from different backgrounds into one big community to talk and accumulate unnecessary items. Here we take pride in goods that have absolutely no use, and waste your money that could go to a good cause (visit the 'Debt' page in the 'My Account' page). Our specialized users can put items they no longer want up for sale, and then everyone else can bid on that item, until the sales close. Bidding here initiates deep conversation, and can spark relationships that last for a lifetime (in other cases it just leads to heated arguments, but our philosophy here is 'You can't win it all!'). We hope you enjoy our site, and feel free to contact our staff at jultep830@gmail.com.
